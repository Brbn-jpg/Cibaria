Privacy Policy

What Information We Collect

We collect following information:

  • Account Information: username, email address.
  • Usage Data: history of saved recipes, favorite lists, comments, and ratings.
  • Device Information: browser type, IP address.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and improve our services, such as organizing recipes and customizing features.
  • Personalize your experience, including recommending recipes based on your preferences.
  • Communicate about your account, including updates and notifications.
  • Analyze usage patterns on our platform to enhance its functionality.

Sharing Information

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share information with:

  • Service Providers supporting the operation of Cibaria, such as hosting and analytics.
  • Law Enforcement Authorities if required by law.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data. However, as this is a passion project, we cannot guarantee complete security.


Your Rights

You have rights to:

  • Access your personal data stored on our platform.
  • Correct inaccurate or outdated information.
  • Request the deletion of your account along with saved data.
  • Opt out of receiving promotional communications.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about privacy, please contact us at: support@cibaria.edu
